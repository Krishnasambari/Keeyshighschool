About Keeys High School

Founded in 1993, Keeys High School has been a cornerstone of education in Mattewada, Warangal. We follow the Telangana State Syllabus and offer classes from Nursery to 10th Standard, providing a strong foundation for every child's future.

Our institution is committed to academic excellence, character building, and all-round development. With dedicated teachers and modern teaching methods, we create an environment where students thrive academically and personally.
